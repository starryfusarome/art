Types of art

Visual arts encompass a broad spectrum of artistic expressions that primarily rely on visual elements, such as shapes, colors, lines, and textures, to convey meaning and evoke emotions.

Visual Arts

Visual arts encompass a broad spectrum of artistic expressions that primarily rely on visual elements, such as shapes, colors, lines, and textures, to convey meaning and evoke emotions. Visual arts encompass drawing, painting, sculpture, photography, graphic design, and other forms of creative expression.

Performing Arts

These arts emphasize the use of the body, voice, and sometimes props or scenery to convey artistic expression. Performing arts encompass theater, dance, music, opera, circus arts, puppetry, and so on.

Literary Arts

Literary arts encompass a wide range of creative writing and storytelling forms that use language as the primary medium of expression. Literary arts encompass poetry, prose fiction, drama, nonfiction, creative writing, and so on.

Digital Arts

Digital arts encompass a diverse range of artistic practices that utilize digital technologies to create visual, auditory, and interactive artworks. Digital arts encompass digital painting and drawing, graphic design, 3D modeling and animation, digital photography and photo manipulation, graphic design, digital music and sound art, and other forms of creative expression.

Graffiti and Street Art

Graffiti and street art are forms of visual expression that take place in public spaces, often outside the confines of traditional art institutions. It includes graffiti, street art, mural painting, stencil art and different snstallations.

Design

Design encompasses a broad spectrum of creative disciplines focused on problem-solving, communication, and innovation. It is about graphic design, visual communication design, uUser Experience (UX) design, User Interface (UI) design, product design, fashion design, interior design and environmental design.
